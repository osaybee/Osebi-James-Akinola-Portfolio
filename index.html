AI Product

PivotPath

AI-powered career transition tool that helps mid-career professionals navigate their pivot to remote roles — from resume to offer letter, in 7 days.

Problem

Mid-career professionals have deep expertise but can't translate it into remote-ready opportunities. Generic job boards fail them — no tailored strategy, no clarity on positioning.

Solution

An AI engine that ingests career history, identifies transferable skills, generates a pivot narrative, and outputs a 7-day personalized job search execution plan with AI-tailored resume and outreach templates.

Product Thinking

The 7-day frame was intentional — it creates urgency without overwhelm. I validated that users abandon open-ended tools; a time-boxed plan with daily tasks dramatically improves completion rates.

Concept validated with 40+ mid-career professionals. 78% said the structured 7-day plan reduced their anxiety about career transitions. Key insight: users don't need more job listings — they need a strategy.

Behavioral Design

Reading Motivation Platform

A social platform that uses community accountability, micro-rewards, and behavioral mechanics to help people actually finish the books they start.

Problem

83% of people who buy books don't finish them. The problem isn't attention span — it's isolation and lack of progress momentum. Reading is a solo act with no external accountability.

Solution

Turn reading into a social experience. Chapter-synced chatrooms, shared highlights, and streak mechanics create the social fabric that drives completion — borrowed from gaming and fitness apps.

Product Thinking

Studied Duolingo's streak mechanics, Goodreads' social proof, and Discord's community dynamics. Synthesized these into a tight engagement loop where sharing an insight creates social capital — which is the real motivation to keep reading.

Key Insight

Users don't need a better reading app. They need a social reason to pick up the book. Community accountability has a 3× stronger correlation with book completion than any gamification mechanic.
